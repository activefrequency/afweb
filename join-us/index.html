Join Us!

Software Craftspersons Apply Within

Join a small team of developers who care about their craft and helping businesses solve problems with software.

Technology-wise, we use Python (Django specifically) on the backend, and Vue.js on the frontend, along with the usual assortment of modern deployment scenarios (typically Heroku or AWS). However, we recognize that we live in an ever-changing world and we're not dead-set on you knowing a particular buzzword on day one.

As long as you understand the Web, you'll fit in. If you have recent mobile experience, or some design chops, all the better.

As a team member, you'll be responsible for architecting, building, and supporting web projects for our awesome clients, which include startups, institutions, and small businesses looking to improve the world with software. You won't be entirely on your own, and will have support when you need it, but will have substantial project ownership.

Remote is OK, though we'll ask you to come to Boston once in a while (on our dime).
